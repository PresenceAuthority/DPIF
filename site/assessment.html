Non-Compensatory Logic

Failure of any single Critical Presence Control results in an immediate FAIL determination. No maturity score, pillar strength, or SPC performance compensates for CPC failure. This is intentional — Presence Integrity cannot be negotiated in aggregate.

Assessment Programme

Three levels of assessment

The DPIF assessment programme progresses from self-qualification through to third-party verification and organisational maturity evaluation. The self-assessment is the entry point — it qualifies readiness for formal assessment.

01
Self-Assessment
Operator-conducted evaluation against all 18 DPIF controls using the Control Checklist v2.0 and non-compensatory scoring logic. Immediate determination with gap analysis and remediation guidance.
Audience: Platform operators, compliance teams, developers
Output: Self-assessed determination + gap summary
Status: Available above — no registration required
02
Formal Assessment
Third-party assessment conducted by The Presence Authority. Evidence is reviewed against the Control Checklist v2.0. The Scoring Rubric v1.0 is applied to SPCs. Formal certification is issued on pass.
Audience: Operators who have passed or nearly passed self-assessment
Output: DPIF certification + Scoring Rubric report
Status: Request a consultation →
03
Organisational Maturity Evaluation
Programme-level evaluation assessing governance maturity across all DRRP deployments. Evaluates consistency, policy infrastructure, and systemic risk management rather than individual deployments.
Audience: Organisations with multiple deployments or enterprise programmes
Output: Maturity report + governance recommendations
Status: Enquire via consultation →
